Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT) is an institute of national importance located in Jais, Amethi, Uttar Pradesh, India. Established in 2007, RGIPT is dedicated to the development and dissemination of knowledge in the field of petroleum technology. The institute is named after the former Prime Minister of India, Rajiv Gandhi, and it primarily focuses on providing education and research opportunities in the upstream and downstream sectors of the petroleum and energy industry.
